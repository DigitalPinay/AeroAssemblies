Aero manufactures cables for the garage door installation industry. The following are standard configurations. We can also manufacture many non-standard variations to fit your needs. Call us for a consultation for custom orders or quote.

Extension Spring Cables are generally constructed with a 7X7 cable in diameters of 3/32" or 1/8". The standard configuration is a loop on one end and a bare end on the other. The length of the cable is measured from the bearing surface of the loop to the end of the cable. Cables are ordered in pairs.
 
Torsion Spring Cables are constructed with 7x7 or 7X19 cable in diameters of 3/32", 1/8", 5/32" or 3/16". Our standard configuration is a loop on one end and a stop on the other end.
Plain Loop Loop w/ Thimble Loop w/ Cable Spool

Looped ends can be plain or reinforced with a thimble or cable spool eye. Stops are made of aluminum. Cable lengths are measured between the bearing surfaces as shown in Figure 1. Cables are ordered in pairs.
Figure 1 - Cable measurement
 
Commercial Torsion Spring Cables are generally constructed with 7X19 cable in diameters of 3/32", 5/32" or 3/16". The cables are reinforced to handle industrial or heavy duty applications. The standard configuration is a thimbled loop on one end and 2 stops (floating and fixed) on the other end as shown in Figure 2. Cable lengths are measured from the bearing surfaces of the loop and the fixed stop. Cables are ordered in pairs.
Figure 2 - Commercial Torsion assembly with double stop
 
Lock Cables are constructed with a 3/32" 7x7 cable. Our standard configuration is with a stop on one end and a bare end on the other.
