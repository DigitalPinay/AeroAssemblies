THIMBLES
Thimbles and Cable Spools are used to reinforce the inter-bearing surface of a loop in a wire rope assembly. Galvanized carbon steel thimbles are the most common.
CABLE SPOOLS
Cable spools are also used with round pins or bolts and are better suited for applications requiring a tight fit around the pivot. Bronze and aluminum are stocked for the use in assembly
WIRE ROPE CLIPS
Wire Rope Clips are used to fasten or fabricate loops in the field. When used properly, these components offer a diverse and convenient method to manufacture assemblies in the field. User assumes all risks and responsibilities when using these parts.

BALL ENDS
Stainless balls for use when a round end is required. We stock 3/64" and 1/16" sizes.

BALL STEMS
Ball Stems have a hexagonal crimped shoulder.

 
BALL SHANKS
Ball Shanks are used in applications where a steel shank is required. They are available in both steel/zinc and stainless steels.
ROUND CLEVIS PLUGS
Use in place of eye fittings where weight distribution is a factor.
