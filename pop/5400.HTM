5400 SERIES
The most powerful tool in the POP line, the 5400 can set even the strongest high-strength rivets including 1/4" stainless steel. Building on the design and ergonomic advances embodied in the 5200 series tools, the 5400 series features light trigger action, ease of handling and an air line attached at the centerline for less resistance to operator movement. The long stroke version (5400LS) provides a 1" stroke for setting longer and more specialized rivets. Tools are available with a standard mandrel deflector (PRT5400, PRT5400LS) or with a mandrel collection system (MCS5400, MCS5400LS).
