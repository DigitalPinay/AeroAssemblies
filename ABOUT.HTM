Mission Statement: Aero Assemblies, Inc. is dedicated to being:

  • The Midwest's leading supplier for eyeleting and grommeting assembly services and sales.
  • A leading manufacturer of custom cable and wire rope assemblies.
  • A competitive provider of import sourcing, consulting and brokerage services.

Our goal is to maximize product value for our customers, create a positive and productive work environment for our employees and a profitable investment for our stockholders.

Quality Statement: We at Aero Assemblies, Inc. take great pride in the end products we sell and the service we give. It is to this end that we strive to make every effort to:

  • Ensure that any product sold or manufactured by Aero Assemblies is of the highest quality in materials and workmanship
  • Apply careful and attentive methods of manufacture
  • Provide excellent customer service.

We likewise expect our vendors not only to share our goal of quality goods, but also to take proactive steps to ensure that this quality is maintained from vendor to Aero Assemblies to our customer. Aero Assemblies is currently implementing quality policies and processes in an effort to further our goal of ISO9000 Registry by 2005 or earlier.
