Determining Eyelets

Determing Size - With a wide selection of eyelets, the function of the eyelet will help determine the eyelet diameter to be used. Is the eyelet to reinforce a hole, used as a pivot or as decoration?

Grip Range - Eyelet grip is the thickess which can be held betwen the flange and the setting and will vary with the material being eyeleted.

With all these factors, let our representatives help you select the proper size for your application
