Torsion Spring Cables are constructed with 7x7 or 7X19 cable in diameters of 3/32", 1/8", 5/32" or 3/16". Our standard configuration is a loop on one end and a stop on the other end.

Plain Loop Loop w/ Thimble Loop w/ Cable Spool

Looped ends can be plain or reinforced with a thimble or cable spool eye. Stops are made of aluminum. Cable lengths are measured between the bearing surfaces. Cables are ordered in pairs.
