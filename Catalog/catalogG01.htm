Extension Spring Cables are generally constructed with a 7X7 cable in diameters of 3/32" or 1/8". The standard configuration is a loop on one end and a bare end on the other. The length of the cable is measured from the bearing surface of the loop to the end of the cable. Cables are ordered in pairs.
