Aero Assebmlies, Inc. was founded in November 1972 in Minneapolis by Robert Winick and was bought in June 1983 by Mrs. Tranquilina Winick. Aero Assemblies, Inc. is engaged in the manufacturing of wire rope and cable assemblies to custom specifications of customer in a wide variety of industries rangin from garage door manufacturers to windows maufacturer to exercise equipment manufactureres. Aero Assemblies, Inc. also provides import and distribution of hardware and fasteners. Aero Assemblies, Inc. also provides eyelet sales and eyeleting servies to the printing industry for bindery and design purposes.
