We stock a large range of standard eyelets with various hole diameters and barrel lengths. Our eyelets range from a hole diatmeter of 1/16" up to 5/16". The barrel lengths range from .093" to .437". All eyelets are available in brass and some are available in either nickel or black. If you did not find an eyelet for your application, please call and we can help you with your search. Availability of longer eyelets can be advised upon request. Painted eyelets in PMS colors are available by special order.
