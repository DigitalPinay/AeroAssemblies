Cable Guard Kit Instructions
Descriptions | Parts List | Instructions
1. Using the Cable Cutter, cut the Cable to desired length, making sure to allow for loops. Place one end of the cable through one side of the Cable End Sleeve then through the D-Ring & Clip and form a loop inserting the cable end back through the other side of the sleeve. Allow the cable to protrude slightly through the end of the sleeve.
2. Using the Crimper, crimp the sleeve twice, once on each end of the sleeve. Repeat this process on the other end of the cable attaching th 1" Round Ring.
3. To secure merchandise, place the end of the cable with the D-Ring through the handle on the merchandise and then through the 1" Round Ring on the other end.
4. Then place the 6-32 AVSAFE Screw through
the Finishing Washer and through the small hole in the D-Ring & Clip. Thread the 6-32 Well Nut onto the AVSAFE Screw with the flange toward the D-Ring. Thread this only a few turns so that it is held on the screw but not distorted.
5. Place the Well Nut into any 1/4" hole in the shelf or store fixture (holes may be round, diamond shaped or square, but the Well Nut should have a snug fit before setting if it is to hold properly). 6. Using the AVSAFE Screw Driver, turn the
screw until the Well Nut is securely in place. This may require quite a few turns since the Well Nut is rather long.
7. The Well Nut can be removed and reused over and over as long as the rubber does not show signs of
damage.
