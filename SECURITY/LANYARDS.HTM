Security Lanyards

Aero Assemblies, Inc., produces over 2 million lanyards per year for our customers. We stock a variety of sizes and colors and we have the ablitily to produce lanyards to your specifications.

 


Our stocked security lanyards are manufactured with the following specifications
  • A 1/2" loop on one end and a 3/4" loop on the other end.
  • 3/64" 1X19 galvanized aircraft cable coated with PVC to .075"
  • Our crimps are smooth and clean with no exposed cable
